About Us

A SHORT HISTORY OF THE SCHOOL

Education brings civilization in Man. It brings many changes in Man. It brings behavioural changes and also develops his knowledge. It develops his knowledge. It develops good habits in Man. An educated man is respected by all. To promote new ideas, new views to update himself education is very essential.

In seventies, studying in English medium schools was very difficult for the average and poor families. Our school Little Flower English Medium was started for these people only. At first it was started with two sections L.K.G. and U.K.G. of Tamil Medium with only forty students. On 1971, it was grown to English Medium. Now nearly 1400 students are studying in our school.

Our motto is ‘LEAD KINDLY LIGHT’ we bring out the children from darkness to the path of light through education. ‘I am the way and the Light’ as per Jesus Words, we teaches, follow our motto in our daily life. We give importance to discipline and human values.

Our School is equipped with well and fine class rooms, Laboratories, computers and games room. All the facilities were set to promote education in a better way.

Our school has Kinder Garten and Play School for kids, provided with all types of rides, to bring them up in a play way method.

To bring equality among the students, all week days our boys wear Blue-Blue Uniform. On Wednesdays they wear white and White uniform.

In our school campass itself we conduct Dance class, Music class, Karate Class, Band, Chess and Drawing class to improve and bring out the talents hidden in our boys.

In sports and games, a special coaching is being given to the students in the morning as well as in the evening.

For the betterment of the students and to develop their talents opportunities are given to the boys to participate in the various cultural programmes during our sports day celebration, Teacher’s day celebration, Christmas celebration, Feast day celebration, Annual day celebration etc.,
