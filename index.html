About School

A Legacy of Learning Since 1971

Our school started on 12.06.1971 by late Rev. Fr. S.C. Sebastian as the first correspondent with the first H.M. Mrs. M.S. Mary and 40 students. Since then, under dedicated leadership from clergy and the Sisters of Immaculate Conception, Madurai, the institution has grown with strong values, discipline, and service.

At present, Rev. Fr. Maria Susai, VG of Salem Diocese serves as correspondent with Rev. Sr. P. Theresa as principal leadership, continuing a proud tradition of academic progress and character formation.
